Game Tutorial

  • Look at the image containing the energy levels of the atom.
  • Look for the "energy level" and "goal". Based on the image, determine the difference between the two values.
  • Using the arrow keys, move the electron to the photon with the correct energy value.
  • Avoid touching any other photons. If you touch an incorrect photon, you lose!
  • Keep an eye on the time. If you run out of time, you lose!
  • There are 5 levels. As the levels get harder, more incorrect photons appear and the timer gets shorter!
  • If you lose at any point, you have to start over!
  • Beat all 5 levels, and you win!
