Welcome to Electron Mania!
In this game you will control an electron in Neils Bohr's model of the atom.
Your goal is to reach the correct electron to reach the requested energy level before time runs out!

About the Bohr Model:
The Bohr model of the atom was created by Niels Bohr in 1913. Bohr based his model off a formula developed by Johann Jacob Balmer that could be used to calculate wavelengths in the hydrogen spectrum.
