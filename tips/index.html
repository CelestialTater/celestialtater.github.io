Game Tips

  • Take your time on the energy level screen. Make sure you know how much energy you need!
  • Estimation is a good technique. Most of the time you won't need to know the exact value you need, just the general area.
  • Try to find a path to the photon that will involve very few of the incorrect photons
  • Be careful not to stand still! The photons move randomly and an incorrect one could move into you!
  • Keep an eye on the time. If you run out of time, you lose!
  • There are 5 levels. As the levels get harder, more incorrect photons appear and the timer gets shorter!
  • As the time gets shorter, you will need to start moving faster! Remember that you cannot speed up your movement.
